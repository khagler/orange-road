Ray Don (aka Chet Dixon) plays the harmonica at the Room 5 Lounge in Los Angeles on December 9th 2006, accompanied by Billy Clem (aka Mike Freas) on guitar and Slim Hawkins (aka Carson Cohen) on bass. Partially visible are Cousin Johnny on banjo and A-Train Hawkins (aka Adam Marcello) on drums.
