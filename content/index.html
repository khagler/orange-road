--- title: What does the name mean? ---

Orange Road is a reference to one of my favorite stories, Kimagure Orange Road. Kimagure means “capricious” or “whimsical” in Japanese, and Orange Road is the name of a (fictional) street in Tokyo where the central character lives.

You won’t find much in the way of Kimagure Orange Road stuff here, in part because I don’t want to invite legal trouble, but mostly because plenty of other people have already done a much better job than I could.
